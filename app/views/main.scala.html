About

The idea for this simple yet powerful application has been inspired by the famous book "The Seven Habits of Highly Effective People". Trello is such a great and useful tool that it is used as the store of the items - in that sense "First things first" is just another view on your Trello cards providing an effective and efficient way to organize your tasks.
